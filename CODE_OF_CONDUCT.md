# Code of Conduct

This project uses a simple contribution standard: be respectful, stay on topic,
and help keep the issue tracker safe for people who may be handling private
local profile data.

## Expected Behavior

- Use clear technical language and assume good intent.
- Keep bug reports focused on reproducible behavior.
- Redact private credentials, account data, and session content before posting.
- Accept maintainer decisions about project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Posting another person's private data or credentials.
- Pressuring contributors to share secrets, tokens, profile archives, or private
  conversations.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers ask to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments that expose secrets or violate
this code. They may also close issues, lock conversations, or block accounts
when needed to protect contributors and users.
